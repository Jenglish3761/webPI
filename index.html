About

The Barbell PI device is a exercise aid designed to track motion, speed, and force of a barbell during exercise and display this in a useable way to our clients.

The device uses a combination of accelerometer and gyroscope data in order to determine the device's relative position in 2D space. This data is collected on a microprocessor in the device and transmitted to the user's smartphone or PC that is connected through this site.

Data is presented to the user through multiple clean and easy to read graphs

Target Audience

The target audiance for the Barbell PI stretches from casual lifters to trainers and olympians.

The device allows casual lifters to improve form, advanced/olympic lifters to track velocities and finer details, and trainers or physiotherapists can use the data to monitor their clients.
